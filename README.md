# battle-snakes

Back in 2017, when *Snake.io* was becoming popular, I built my own local two-player version to play with friends. It follows the classic gameplay—if your snake's head hits another snake's body, you lose—but also introduces a few unique mechanics.

**Poison Food**
Eating poisoned food cuts your snake's length in half. If its length drops below 2, the snake dies. However, after eating 5 poisoned foods, the snake transforms into a **Poison Snake**. Poison Snakes can only eat poisoned food, but each one increases their length by 3 instead of the normal +1.

**Iron Head**
Iron-colored food occasionally appears on the map. Eating it grants a temporary **Iron Head** ability, allowing the snake to crash through another snake's body and cut it at the point of impact.

2017 年，《Snake.io》开始流行起来时，我为了和朋友一起玩，开发了这款本地双人版游戏。它保留了经典玩法——如果蛇头撞到另一条蛇的身体，就会失败——同时也加入了一些独特的机制。
毒食物（Poison Food）
吃下毒食物后，蛇的长度会减半；如果长度不足 2，则会直接死亡。不过，当一条蛇累计吃下 5 个毒食物后，它会变成一条毒蛇（Poison Snake）。毒蛇只能吃毒食物，但每吃一个都会增加 3 点长度，而普通食物只能增加 1 点。
铁头（Iron Head）
地图上会随机出现铁色食物。吃下后，蛇会在一段时间内获得**铁头（Iron Head）**能力，可以直接撞穿另一条蛇的身体，并从碰撞的位置将其截断。
